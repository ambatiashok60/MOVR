# ADR-NNNN: Decision title

- Status: proposed
- Date: YYYY-MM-DD
- Owners: team or role
- Supersedes: none

## Context

What forces a decision now? Include constraints, quality attributes, and evidence.

## Decision

State the decision precisely, including boundaries and what is deliberately not
being decided.

## Alternatives considered

Describe credible alternatives and why they were not selected.

## Consequences

List positive, negative, operational, security, cost, and reversibility effects.

## Validation

What tests, metrics, or review will demonstrate that the decision works?

## Rollout and rollback

How is the change introduced safely, and what restores the prior state?
